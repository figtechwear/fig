Movement & Materials

At FIG, movement is not just a result — it’s the origin. Every stitch, seam, and silhouette begins with a study of the body in motion through urban environments.

Engineered for Urban Dynamics

Our garments are built for transition — from concrete streets to quiet studios, from transit lines to creative zones. We craft each piece to adapt with you, using mapped flexibility zones, breathable structures, and form-fitting durability.

Materials of Precision

We partner with performance textile innovators to source fabrics that are ultralight, moisture-adaptive, and long-wearing. From bio-based nylon to recycled stretch blends, every material is chosen for its longevity, feel, and environmental integrity.

Minimal Waste. Maximal Purpose.

Sustainability isn’t a feature — it’s a foundation. We apply modular construction, limit dye baths, and reduce offcuts through precision patterning. Less excess. More essence.
